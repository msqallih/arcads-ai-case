## 📊 The Question

Which paywall discount performs better?
- **Test 1:** 10% off first month (Jan 1-10, 2026)
- **Test 2:** 30% off first month (Jan 28 - Feb 10, 2026)

---

## 🏆 The Answer

### **30% Discount Wins Decisively**

| Metric | 10% Test | 30% Test | Winner |
|--------|----------|----------|--------|
| **Conversion Lift** | +4.29% | **+48.24%** | 30% |
| **LTV Lift** | -13.31% | **+9.06%** | 30% |
| **Marketing Conv. Lift** | +23% | **+201%** | 30% |
| **Cost per Purchase** | €185 | **€196** | 30% |

---

## 📈 Key Numbers

### Product Performance (30% Test)
- **Sample Size:** 22,500 workspaces per cohort
- **Control (A):** 364 conversions (1.63%)
- **Variant (B):** 546 conversions (2.41%)
- **Lift:** +48.24% (highly significant)
- **LTV Impact:** +9.06% (€2.28 → €2.48)

### Marketing Performance (30% Test Period)
- **Baseline Conversion:** 6.37%
- **During Test:** 19.17%
- **Lift:** +201%
- **Cost per Purchase:** €223 → €196 (-12%)

---

## 💡 Why 30% Wins

1. **Massive Conversion Boost:** Nearly 50% more conversions
2. **Positive LTV:** More customers offset lower per-customer revenue
3. **Better Marketing ROI:** 12% cheaper to acquire customers
4. **Statistical Significance:** Large sample size confirms results
5. **Validated by Marketing Data:** Both product and marketing data align
