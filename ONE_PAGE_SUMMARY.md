# 📊 ARCADS A/B TEST - ONE PAGE SUMMARY

---

## 🎯 THE QUESTION
Which paywall discount drives better results: 10% or 30% off first month?

---

## 🏆 THE ANSWER: 30% DISCOUNT WINS

### Product Performance
| Metric | Control (A) | 30% Discount (B) | Lift |
|--------|-------------|------------------|------|
| Workspaces | 22,384 | 22,649 | - |
| Conversions | 364 (1.63%) | 546 (2.41%) | **+48.24%** ⭐ |
| LTV per Workspace | €2.28 | €2.48 | **+9.06%** ⭐ |
| Avg First Payment | €106.83 | €77.71 | -27.3% |
| Renewal Rate | 6.88% | 6.02% | -12.5% |

### Marketing Performance
| Period | Conversion Rate | Cost per Purchase | Lift |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|------|
| Baseline | 6.37% | €223.05 | - |
| 30% Test | 19.17% | €195.90 | **+201% / -12%** ⭐ |

---

## 📈 COMPARISON: 10% vs 30%

| Metric | 10% Discount | 30% Discount | Winner |
|--------|-------------|--------------|--------|
| Conversion Lift | +4.29% | **+48.24%** | 30% |
| LTV Lift | -13.31% | **+9.06%** | 30% |
| Marketing Conv. | +23% | **+201%** | 30% |
| Sample Size | 24,750 | 22,500 | Both large |

**Conclusion:** 30% discount outperforms 10% discount across all metrics

---

## 💡 KEY INSIGHTS

1. **Massive Conversion Boost:** 48% more conversions with 30% discount
2. **Positive LTV Impact:** Volume gains offset lower per-customer revenue (+9%)
3. **Marketing Efficiency:** Cost per purchase improved by 12% (€223 → €196)
4. **Statistical Significance:** Large sample sizes (~22,500 per cohort) confirm results
5. **Cross-Validation:** Both product and marketing data show same trend
6. **Retention Opportunity:** 6% renewal rate needs improvement (target: 15%+)
